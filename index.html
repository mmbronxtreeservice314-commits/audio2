Frequently Asked Questions

What does an audio visual consultant do?
An audio visual consultant helps businesses and individuals plan, design, and implement AV systems that meet their specific communication and operational needs. At NYC IT Tech, our audio visual consultants start by understanding your goals — whether it's improving conference room meetings, setting up a multi-room audio system, or creating an immersive presentation environment. We assess your space, recommend the right equipment, manage procurement, oversee installation, and ensure everything is configured and working perfectly. We serve as your trusted technology partner from concept through completion, saving you time, money, and frustration.
What is included in a conference room AV installation?
A comprehensive conference room AV installation typically includes displays or projectors for clear presentations, a professional audio system with microphones and speakers for crystal-clear communication, video conferencing equipment for remote participants, and a centralized control system that ties everything together. At NYC IT Tech, we also handle all the cabling, mounting, and configuration — ensuring the system is intuitive for every user in the room. We can integrate with popular platforms like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet so your team can connect seamlessly with no technical headaches.
How long does a CCTV or surveillance camera installation take?
Installation time depends on the size of your property and the number of cameras being installed. A basic system for a small office or apartment can typically be completed in a single day. Larger commercial properties or multi-camera systems covering an entire building may take 2–4 days. During our initial consultation, we assess your property, discuss your security needs, and provide a detailed timeline. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily operations while ensuring every camera is properly positioned, connected, and tested before we leave.
Can I access my security cameras remotely from my phone?
Absolutely. Remote access is one of the most valuable features of a modern surveillance system, and it's something we set up for every client. As long as you have an internet connection, you can view live and recorded footage from your security cameras on any device — smartphone, tablet, or computer — from anywhere in the world. We configure secure remote viewing access as part of every CCTV installation, and we walk you through how to use it so you're always in control of your property, whether you're at the office, traveling, or at home.

Yes — our relationship with clients doesn't end at installation. NYC IT Tech provides ongoing IT and tech support, system maintenance, troubleshooting, and upgrades for all the systems we install. We take pride in being available for our loyal customers at any time of day, ensuring your AV, network, or security infrastructure is always running at its best.
